# Woodstock

Three days of peace and music in upstate New York draw 400,000 people. Jimi Hendrix, Janis Joplin, The Who, Jefferson Airplane, and Crosby, Stills, Nash & Young perform. The Beatles are not there — they had already stopped touring.
